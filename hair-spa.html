Hair extensions

It is not always easy to achieve lusciously long hair. This is because hair growth and structure are affected by various environmental factors, including stress, chemical treatments, natural disposition and eating habits. Hair must be constantly trimmed, and the dream of having long, healthy natural hair can only come true with plenty of care and patience.

Make this dream a reality sooner and more conveniently with natural hair extensions from AERNI Bern & Hairdreams. In just a few hours, you’ll have the hair length and thickness you desire – perfectly resembling real hair. It can even be styled virtually any way you wish.
